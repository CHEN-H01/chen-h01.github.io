## About Me

I am a Second-year PhD student at [The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK)](https://www.cuhk.edu.hk) supervised by [Prof. Pheng-Ann Heng](https://appsrv.cse.cuhk.edu.hk/~pheng/index.php) and [Prof. Chi-Wing Fu](https://www.cse.cuhk.edu.hk/~cwfu/). 
My research interest lies in building a robotic foundation model that realizes an intelligent robot architecture with the capabilities to 'Understand, Think, and Act'. Therefore, my current research directions mainly include <span style="color:#39c">Vision-Language-Action Models, World-Action Models, and Multi-Modal Learning</span>. 
Feel free to reach out - I'm always open to discussions and potential collaborations! 😄
